# The Mouvelian calendar

(named after Grand Patriarch Mouvel, the first high priest of the Church of Dwayna)
begins counting years from the moment the gods left Tyria.
This event is known as the Exodus.
Years before this date are labeled BE (Before the Exodus).
Years after this date are AE (After the Exodus).
Years prior to the year 1 AE count down, getting smaller as they get closer to the time of the Exodus
(just as they do in the Gregorian calendar).

There are 4 seasons and 360 days in the Mouvelian year:

| Season                 | Alignment | Days    |
| ---------------------- | --------- | ------- |
| Season of the Zephyr   | Air       |   1- 90 |
| Season of the Phoenix  | Flame     |  91-180 |
| Season of the Scion    | Water     | 181-270 |
| Season of the Colossus | Earth     | 271-360 |
